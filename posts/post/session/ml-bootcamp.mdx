### Overview

The ML Bootcamp, held on February 28, 2024, was a dynamic and immersive learning experience designed to empower participants with the essential skills and knowledge required to make a significant impact in the community through machine learning.

### Session Highlights

- **Hands-on Workshops:** Participants engaged in hands-on workshops covering various aspects of machine learning, including data preprocessing, model training, and evaluation.
  
- **Interactive Sessions:** The bootcamp featured interactive sessions where participants collaborated, shared insights, and tackled real-world machine learning challenges.

- **Expert Guidance:** Experienced mentors provided expert guidance, offering valuable insights and tips to help participants overcome obstacles and enhance their understanding of machine learning concepts.
