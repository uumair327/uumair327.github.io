## Features

- Puzzle & Functional game
- Engine used: Unity
- Enhances understanding of colors

## How to Play

- Press the 'gray path' already placed to change its direction.
- Press the desired color of 'path' and touch the screen to place the path at that position.
- Once placed, pressing the path again allows you to change its direction.
