## 5. Exporting

Click on the Gradle option on the right side of the IDE.

![1](/img/2017-7-20-LemonadeAPI-func/27.PNG)

Select :app/other/exportJar.

![1](/img/2017-7-20-LemonadeAPI-func/28.PNG)

After completion, you'll find AndroidPlugin.jar in the project/app/release directory.

Common reasons for the jar file not appearing:

1. Choosing something other than :app/other/exportJar.
2. Inconsistency in compilation targets between project/build.gradle and project/app/build.gradle, often caused by blindly copying the provided build.gradle script.

## 6. Moving AndroidPlugin.jar

![1](/img/2017-7-20-LemonadeAPI-func/30.PNG)

Move the released AndroidPlugin.jar file to Unity-Project-Path/Assets/Plugins/Android/.
